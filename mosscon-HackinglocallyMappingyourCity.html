House Numbers The blood of osm, needed for all applications, most time consuming to collect. See OSM Wiki on Bing That is why we import them from the local municipal and county governments!
Parcels Well we dont get "Numbers of houses", but really numbers of the Parcel, often people give numbers that are unknown to the city. Cities care about taxes not post. We import the parcels which can be searched via Nominatim even if not visible on the map. Also you can import the centers of the parcel and move it into place by hand.
